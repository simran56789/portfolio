Digital Electronics & Computer Architechture

Audio Switch Circuit

Overview: In this project, we've made a simple audio switch circuit using easily accessible components . This circuit utilizes sound-sensitive components to detect the distinct sound-patterns , translating them into electrical signals. Furthermore , We began by setting up the PCB, attaching power and ground connections to the components . Next, we connected the timing components, including resistors and capacitors, which determine the blink rate of our LED or buzzer. Two NPN transistors act as electronic switches, controlling the indicator. By linking everything in a feedback loop, we transformed our circuit into a sound based automation circuit. When powered on, the LED blinks on and off, its speed adjustable by altering the resistor and capacitor values. Any auditory response is caught on by the circuit and it responds in positive. Furthermore this is a sound sensitive circuit with adjustable LED blink rate , responding positively to distinct sound patterns creating an interactive and dynamic display.
